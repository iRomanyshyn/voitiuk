## Anna Voitiuk

Printmaker and graphic artist from Ukraine.

### About

Anna Voitiuk works primarily with monochrome and highly detailed print techniques. Her visual language often combines poetic symbolism, animals, and surreal narrative scenes.

### Name variants

In different sources, the surname appears as **Voitiuk** and **Vojtiuk**.  
These are transliteration variants; both can refer to the same artist profile.

### For curators, galleries, and media

- Primary English form on this site: **Anna Voitiuk**
- Acceptable variant: **Anna Vojtiuk**
- Location: Ukraine
- Field: Printmaking / Graphic art
